Shaping Future Leaders Through Excellence in Education

Empowering students with knowledge, creativity, and confidence for a successful future.

Proxima International Academy (PIA) is a progressive co-educational school established and operated by highly qualified, dedicated and motivated team of school leaders. It offer life and soft skill based learning and behavior modification opportunities to each students to identify and explore their unique innate potentials and abilities to live a better and meaningful life.

VISION

Proxima International Academy aspires to be a premium school to nurture students' individual strengths, talents and intelligences, and transforming them to be a globally competent and responsible citizen.

MISSION

Proxima International Academy provides opportunities to the students to explore, identify and enhance their own innate potential and strength for their holistic development through a blend of latest tools, techniques, philosophies and pedagogies scaffolded by skilled and highly qualified intellectual resources.
